Forest Health Citizen Science Community

Welcome

Welcome to the webpage for the Forest Health Citizen Science Community. The sole purpose of this page is to share information relavent to the community.

Join the community

Anyone working in the space between forest health and society is welcome to join.

Subscribe to the mailing-list by visiting the Groups.io page (https://groups.io/g/ForestHealthCitSci) or by emailing the subscibe email listed below:

  • Group Email Addresses
  • Post: ForestHealthCitSci@groups.io
  • Subscribe: ForestHealthCitSci+subscribe@groups.io
  • Unsubscribe: ForestHealthCitSci+unsubscribe@groups.io
  • Group Owner: ForestHealthCitSci+owner@groups.io
  • Help: ForestHealthCitSci+help@groups.io
